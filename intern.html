Humanarium Summer Internship

Help grow a club for humans — its community and its voice — across July and August.

Founding Internship New York · Hybrid July–August 2026

Humanarium is a young New York initiative protecting human connection in the age of AI. We host salons and screenings, publish a Journal, and gather field notes from the science of being together. Across July and August, we're inviting a founding intern to help build it with us — close to the work, and close to the people making it.

What you'd work on

  • Social media — shape and grow our voice across platforms: plan posts, write captions, and keep the feed human, not noisy.
  • Community building — welcome new people, tend our members, and help our gatherings feel like somewhere you belong.
  • Content into posts — turn our events and Journal pieces into small stories that travel.
  • Light event help — lend a hand before and during our salons and screenings.

Who you are

  • Curious about AI and what it's doing to the way we connect.
  • A clear, careful writer and communicator.
  • Organized and self-directed — comfortable owning a task end to end.
  • Warm with people; you make a room feel better.
  • Bonus: New York–based, and fluent in social media, community, and storytelling.

The terms

  • Part-time, roughly 10–15 hours a week.
  • July–August 2026, with flexible hours.
  • New York–based, hybrid — some in person, some remote.
  • A founding internship with a small stipend, plus mentorship, a strong reference, and course credit where possible.

How to apply

Tell us who you are and why Humanarium. It takes about five minutes — no formal cover letter required.

Applications are open. We read every one.
